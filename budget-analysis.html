Budget Analysis

In-depth evaluation of production budgets to improve cost efficiency, funding alignment, and financial feasibility.

Service Overview

Budget Analysis focuses on reviewing and evaluating planned production costs before filming begins. A well-structured budget is critical for funding approvals, cost control, and rebate eligibility.

Unica Media works closely with producers and financiers to identify inefficiencies, assess cost risks, and align budgets with industry benchmarks and funding expectations.

What We Analyze

  • Line-by-line budget review
  • Cost benchmarking against comparable productions
  • Identification of high-risk cost heads
  • Funding gap and cash-flow assessment
  • Alignment with rebate and incentive criteria
  • Feasibility review for studios and investors

Who This Service Is For

  • • Producers in pre-production or funding stage
  • • Studios assessing project feasibility
  • • Financiers and investors reviewing budgets
  • • OTT platforms evaluating commissioning proposals
