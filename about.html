About Unica Media

A specialist consultancy delivering financial clarity, compliance confidence, and global incentive optimization for film and media productions.

Who We Are

Unica Media is a specialized advisory firm focused exclusively on the financial and regulatory aspects of film, OTT, and advertising productions. Founded under the umbrella of Mr. Anil Sekhri, the firm brings decades of experience in production finance, governance, and compliance.

Our work spans production audits, project accounting, budget analysis, cost reporting, compliance oversight, and global film rebate advisory—supporting productions from pre-production through final audit and closure.

Leadership

Mr. Anil Sekhri

Founder & Principal Advisor

“Strong productions are built on creative vision supported by financial clarity, discipline, and compliance.”

Mr. Anil Sekhri brings decades of experience in financial governance, audit, and advisory services, with a strong focus on the film, entertainment, and media production ecosystem.

His professional background spans production audits, financial structuring, compliance oversight, and advisory engagements for feature films, OTT content, and large-scale advertising productions. He has worked closely with producers, studios, financiers, and production houses to ensure financial transparency, regulatory compliance, and audit-ready reporting.

Under his leadership, Unica Media was established to provide specialized, end-to-end financial and compliance advisory services tailored specifically to the operational realities of film and media productions—bridging the gap between creative execution and financial discipline.

Our Mission

To be a trusted financial partner to the entertainment industry by delivering transparent project accounting, rigorous audits, and optimized rebate solutions that protect budgets, ensure compliance, and enhance production value worldwide.
