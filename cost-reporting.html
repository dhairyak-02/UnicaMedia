Service Overview

Cost Reporting converts production accounting data into clear, structured, and periodic financial reports that provide real-time visibility into production spend.

Unica Media ensures reports are consistent, transparent, and aligned with the expectations of producers, studios, financiers, and auditors.

What We Deliver

  • Budget vs actual variance analysis
  • Cash-flow tracking and reporting
  • Financier- and studio-aligned formats
  • Rebate-compatible cost summaries
  • Audit-ready documentation

Who This Service Is For

  • • Ongoing film and OTT productions
  • • Financiers requiring real-time cost visibility
  • • Studios and broadcasters monitoring spend
  • • Producers seeking audit-ready reporting
