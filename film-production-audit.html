What is a Film Production Audit?

A Film Production Audit is a detailed examination of production budgets, accounting records, expenditures, and contractual obligations to ensure financial accuracy, transparency, and compliance.

At Unica Media, our audits are conducted with a deep understanding of real-world production workflows, studio expectations, and financier reporting standards—ensuring audit outcomes that are both accurate and defensible.
